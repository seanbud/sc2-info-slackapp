Sc2-Info

A Slack App for querying up-to-date info about maps, units, structures, and upgrades in the game of StarCraft II.


About


This is a free app for social purposes. It's an open source project; feel free to contribute and improve the app.


Click here to find this project on GitHub.





To Install,

click the stylized button below



Usage


This app will provide your workspace with the slash command(s),



/map help


Show usage information

/map <map name>


Find the closest matching sc2 map and write both name and image into that channel.

Privacy Policy


We don't store your data, period.


We physically can't. We have nowhere to store it. We don't even have a server database to store it. So even if The Queen of England asked nicely to see your data, we wouldn't have anything to show Her Majesty.
